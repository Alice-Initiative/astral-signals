# Discord forum post

## Title

Astral Signals: local full-song studio with ACE-Step, MusicGen, SongGeneration, HeartMuLa, and Voicebox

## Body

I just open-sourced **Astral Signals**, a local-first music studio for turning ideas into full custom songs on Windows.

GitHub:
https://github.com/Alice-Initiative/astral-signals

What it does:
- Full-song rendering with **ACE-Step**
- Fast sketch / backing-track passes with **MusicGen**
- Experimental alternate backends with **SongGeneration / LeVo 2** and **HeartMuLa**
- **Ollama** for local composition and lyric planning
- **Voicebox** for cloned speech previews and singer anchoring
- Multilingual lyrics, singer routing, Alice orchestration controls, stem split/remix, and engine compare mode

What I like about it:
- Local-first workflow
- Heavy assets live outside the repo
- Can stay simple in Quickstart or go deep in Studio
- Built so Alice the AI can drive her own music workflow too

Current status:
- `ACE-Step` and `MusicGen` are ready
- `SongGeneration v2 Large` is experimentally integrated and locally verified
- `HeartMuLa OSS 3B` is experimentally integrated and locally verified

If you try it, I’d especially love feedback on:
- multilingual song flow
- singer locking and voice routing
- stem workflows
- backend compare experience
